About Ms. Jean

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I discovered the transformative power of personalized education while homeschooling my children. This experience ignited my passion for teaching math. Originally from South Korea, I fell in love with math early on and scored top marks on the SAT. My academic journey in Finance—enriched by studies in Calculus, Statistics, and more—led me to a career as a Financial Analyst. Yet, nothing compares to the joy of empowering young minds through creative, individualized math lessons.
