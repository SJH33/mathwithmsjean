Frequently Asked Questions

I specialize in tutoring students in grades K–8. Whether your child needs help with basic arithmetic, understanding core concepts, or exploring more advanced topics like pre-algebra and algebra, I tailor my sessions to their individual needs. My approach aligns with common school curricula—often incorporating elements of the Big Ideas Math curriculum. Upon request, I can also use a different math curriculum - I am familiar with Singapore Math and Math Mammoth.

In a typical session, which generally lasts around an hour, we begin by reviewing your child's current challenges and homework. I then provide clear explanations of difficult concepts and work through targeted practice problems. For grades 3–5, I recommend 60‑minute sessions, while for younger students (grades 2 and below), 45 minutes is ideal. Middle school students benefit from sessions lasting 60–90 minutes to cover more material.

Booking a session is simple—just email me at MathWithMsJean@gmail.com. After a brief consultation to assess your child's needs, we’ll schedule sessions at times that work for you. My fees are competitive and depend on the session length and the level of personalized instruction required. Please reach out for a customized quote.
